About Wags & Whiskers

At Wags & Whiskers, we believe that every pet deserves love, care, and a little extra pampering. Since our founding in 2020, we've dedicated ourselves to providing top-quality grooming, daycare, and boarding services for dogs and cats of all shapes and sizes.

Our mission is to create a safe, fun, and nurturing environment where your furry family members feel right at home. Whether it's a stylish haircut, a relaxing bath, or a weekend stay, Wags & Whiskers is here to help your pets look and feel their best.

Meet Our Owner

Hi, I'm Alex Carter, the proud owner of Wags & Whiskers. Animals have always been my passion, and I started this business to give pets the care and attention they truly deserve. When I'm not at the shop, you can find me volunteering at local shelters or exploring hiking trails with my golden retriever, Max!
