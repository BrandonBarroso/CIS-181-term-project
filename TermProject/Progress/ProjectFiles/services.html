Our Services

Meet & Greet: Complimentary

This is the perfect opportunity to meet you and your pet(s) in person, discuss our services, and determine which options best fit your pet's needs.

Dog Walking: $40 / 35 min

Boost your pet's mental stimulation, reduce stress, and support their overall health with a full walk. Recommended for high-energy dogs and leash-trained puppies six months and older.

Express Walk: $20 / 15 min

Ideal for a quick mid-day potty break. Perfect for elderly dogs or puppies in leash and potty training.

In-home Pet Sitting: Starting at $65

Keep your pets comfy at home with customized care based on their specific needs. Pricing varies depending on the care required.

Check-in Service: $30 per visit

Dogs: Feeding, water refresh, express walk (15 minutes), play/cuddle time, bringing in mail/packages, and watering plants. Medication administration available for an additional fee.

Cats: Feeding, water refresh, litter box monitoring and scooping, play/cuddle time, bringing in mail/packages, and watering plants. Medication administration available for an additional fee.
